Designing the ATtiny412 Hello Board

Steps Image
First I have to download EAGLE and download the fab lab library from the GitHub repo
After that i create a new project/schematic
Next, I can start adding the necessary parts for the ATtiny412 in by clicking the ADD PART and find the relevent parts than click on OK. Components: ATtiny412 chip, Headers, Capacitors, Resistors, 1x3pinhead, etc..
Using DRAW(Net), I connect the parts to the ATtiny412 chip
After connecting the components, I arrange the circuit neatly and keyed in the values fot the capacitors (1micro) and Resistors (1k and 10K)
